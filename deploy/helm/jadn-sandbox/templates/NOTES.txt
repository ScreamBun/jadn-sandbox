JADN Sandbox has been deployed.

To see the pods:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To port-forward locally (containerPort -> local 8080):

  kubectl port-forward deployment/{{ include "jadn-sandbox.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.containerPort }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

Then open:

  http://localhost:8080

If ingress is enabled:

  1. Get minikube IP:
     minikube ip

  2. Add to /etc/hosts:
     <minikube-ip>  jadn-sandbox.local

  3. Open:
     http://jadn-sandbox.local
